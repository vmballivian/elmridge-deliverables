How the Filters Work

Audit any time

Each scraper applies a set of filters to keep the noise out and only push forward leads that look like real carpentry opportunities. Every rejected lead is logged to a "Filtered Out" tab in your spreadsheet with the reason it was cut, so you can audit anything that looks wrong and tell us to relax a rule.

DataBid Filters

Keeps:

General Building project class. Industries: Residential, Commercial, Educational, Medical. Boost keywords like residential, condo, apartment, townhouse, multi-unit, mixed-use, framing, carpentry.

Cuts:

Power Generation, Transportation, Automotive, Pollution Control, Heavy Highway. Descriptions with washroom, pump station, sewage, hvac, roofing, elevator, fire alarm, solar, generator, paving, transit, water treatment.

Ottawa Permits Filters

Keeps:

4+ dwelling units (multi-unit residential), or commercial/institutional buildings with substantial work (construct, tenant fit, renovation, alteration, addition, conversion, interior).

Cuts:

Demolitions, pools, single-family homes (DU under 4). Permits with descriptions like fire alarm, sprinkler, elevator, hvac, roofing, plumbing only, electrical only. Trade subcontractors by company name (plumbing, electrical, hvac, glazing, roofing, paving, painting, flooring).

Ottawa DevApps Filters

Keeps:

Site Plan Control, Zoning By-law Amendment, Plan of Subdivision, Plan of Condominium. Anything mentioning storey, residential, dwelling, units, apartment, condo, townhouse, mixed-use, subdivision.

Cuts:

Parking lots, stormwater, sewer, transit station, light rail, cemetery, solar farm, gas station, car wash, self-storage, telecommunications, cell towers.

Every cut goes to the Filtered Out tab in your spreadsheet with the company, address, and the reason it was rejected. If you spot a real lead in there, tell us which one and we'll loosen the rule.

What Is Next?

The Ottawa pipeline is live. These are the expansions available to increase the volume and geographic reach of leads coming in.

Toronto / GTA Permits

Significant build required. Toronto and surrounding municipalities (Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham) each have separate data portals with different formats. Requires research, new scrapers, and extensive testing. Toronto has 3 to 4 times the construction volume of Ottawa.

Rockland / Surrounding Ottawa

Expand to nearby municipalities and surrounding areas. Separate data sources that need to be researched and built.

LinkedIn Sales Navigator

Process saved search exports for additional contact enrichment. Supplements Apollo with LinkedIn activity data and mutual connections.

Cloud Server

Move scrapers to a dedicated server that runs 24/7 without depending on a local computer. Ensures the schedule never misses a run.
